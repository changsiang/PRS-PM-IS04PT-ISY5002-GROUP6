Medical Disclaimer

This AI-powered Eye diagnosis system was developed by a team from NUS-ISS. The AI is trained on the Rotterdam Ophthalmic Data Repository- Longitudinal diabetic retinopathy screening fundus photos with clinical features labelled by Dr Kelvin Li, an Associate Consultant from the Department of Ophthalmology, Tan Tock Seng Hospital.

This version of the system is currently capable of identifying features from diabetic retinopathy, age-related macular degeneration, epiretinal membrane and choroidal nevus. For more information, suggestions or feedback, please contact kelvin_li@ttsh.com.sg
